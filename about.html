About Us

About US

At JTC Painting, we bring color and craftsmanship to homes and businesses across New York City. As a locally owned and operated company, we pride ourselves on delivering premium painting services with a personal touch. Whether you're refreshing a single room or transforming your entire property, our team of skilled painters approaches every project with precision, care, and a commitment to excellence. With years of hands-on experience in NYC’s diverse architecture and interiors, we know what it takes to deliver a flawless finish that lasts.

We treat every job — big or small — with the same level of professionalism and care. From the moment we arrive for the estimate to the final walkthrough, our focus is on communication, cleanliness, and delivering results you’ll love. No shortcuts, no surprises — just great painting.
